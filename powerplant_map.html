The map below presents a high-level overview of emissions and load (from 2001 onward) of plants in the Western Interconnection. The data is taken from the EPA's public datasets from continuous emissions monitoring systems (CEMS), which report hourly data.

The monthly gross load trend plot displays quartiles of gross load data for a plant or state: the blue line represents the median value, the dark gray area the 25% to 75% quartiles, and the light gray the entire range of values occurring over the month.

The mean hourly emissions plot displays the mean value, over the period of a month, of hourly emissions of three major pollutants covered under the CEMS requirements: SO2, NOx, and CO2.
